Established 1994

A Culinary Voyage From Istanbul

It started with a small clay brick oven and a dream to preserve ancient culinary traditions. Today, Stackly operates a network of specialized cloud kitchens that deliver piping hot gourmet food directly to dinner tables within minutes.

Our dough is slow-fermented for 24 hours, our meat is cut by hand using traditional iron cleavers, and our spices are ground fresh daily, preserving that distinct, savory Turkish aroma.
